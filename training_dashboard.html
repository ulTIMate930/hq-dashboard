⚡ Google Sheets Live Sync

How it works Your Ultra Training Tracker sheet auto-syncs every time you open the dashboard. Just log sessions in the Daily Log tab as normal — the dashboard pulls new entries automatically.
One-time setup (if URL changes): 1. Open your sheet in Google Sheets
2. File → Share → Publish to web
3. Select the "Daily Log" tab → format "CSV"
4. Click Publish → copy the URL → paste below
What syncs: All sessions from the Daily Log tab — distance, pace, watts, stiffness, pain, sleep, protein. New entries are added, existing entries are updated. Your manually-logged workouts are preserved.

Auto-sync: Runs every time you open the dashboard. The ⚡ button above forces an immediate sync.
