Trading Framework — 6 Principles
01 / Define the Edge
Know Why Before You Buy
A repeatable reason the trade should work — market structure, on-chain signal, macro catalyst. If you can't state it in one sentence, you don't have an edge yet.
"If I can't explain why, I'm gambling."
02 / Risk First
Size the Position
Decide max loss before entry. Never risk more than 2% of total portfolio per trade. Use the calculator below. Size the position, not the conviction.
"Size the position, not the conviction."
03 / Cycle Awareness
Trade the Market You're In
Bull: trend-follow, hold longer. Bear: short bias, stay liquid. Sideways: range-trade or step aside. The strategy must match the environment.
"Trade the market you're in."
04 / On-Chain First
Data Before Price
LTH supply, MVRV, funding rates, exchange netflow, open interest. Price is the last thing to move. Glassnode, CryptoQuant, LookIntoBitcoin.
"Follow the coins, not the candles."
05 / Trade Journal
Log Everything
Entry reason, entry price, stop, target, outcome, lesson. Review monthly. Patterns in your losses are tuition.
"If it's not logged, it didn't happen."
06 / Exit Planning
Tops Are a Process
Plan exits before entries. Know your stop before your target. Take profits in tranches as levels hit. Never let a winner fully retrace.
"Top is a process. Exit is a plan."
